\# Product Roadmap



\## Product



Nexus



Universal AI Integration Platform



\---



\# Vision



Enable any AI model, agent, workflow engine, IDE, or application to securely interact with any external service through a unified execution platform.



\---



\# Roadmap Strategy



The roadmap follows four stages:



Stage 1 → Foundation



Stage 2 → Platform



Stage 3 → Ecosystem



Stage 4 → AI Infrastructure



\---
