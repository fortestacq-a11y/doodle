\# Connector Marketplace



\## Purpose



The Connector Marketplace allows developers and organizations to publish, discover, install, and manage Nexus connectors.



The long-term goal is to create an ecosystem where Nexus is not limited by connectors built by the core team.



\---



\# Vision



Instead of:



```text

Nexus Team Builds Every Connector

```



The ecosystem becomes:



```text

Community



↓



Connector SDK



↓



Marketplace



↓



Nexus Platform

```



\---



\# Connector Types



\## Official Connectors



Maintained by Nexus.



Examples:



\* Gmail

\* GitHub

\* Slack

\* Notion



\---



\## Verified Connectors



Built by third parties.



Reviewed by Nexus.



\---



\## Community Connectors



Published without verification.



Use at user's discretion.
